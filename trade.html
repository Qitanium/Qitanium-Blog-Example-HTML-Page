Step 1

Head over to https://waves.exchange and click download while selecting you're OS


Step 2

After creating your account on the client go to "trade" and search "Qitanium" and favorite the token by pressing the star next to the currencies name


Step 3

Next click "Wallet" and find the currency you plan to deposit in this example it's bitcoin


Step 4

Once you're deposit is confirmed head back over to "Trade" click on Qitanium and in the right corner you can now buy and sell Qitanium Coin. Enjoy!
